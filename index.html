Meet Your Physician

Two decades of trusted cardiovascular expertise.

Dr. Babak Alex Vakili is a distinguished interventional cardiologist with additional subspecialty certifications in vascular medicine as well as venous and lymphatic disorders. In private practice in the Orlando area since 2002, he brings over two decades of unparalleled expertise and dedication to cardiovascular medicine.

As former Chief of Cardiology at South Seminole Hospital, he combines clinical leadership with a deeply personal approach — listening carefully, explaining clearly, and building relationships that span decades.

Seminars & Keynote Events

Staying at the leading edge of cardiovascular care.

Dr. Vakili attended the International Symposium on Endovascular Therapy (ISET) in Miami — bringing together leading experts in interventional cardiology, interventional radiology, and vascular surgery for an advanced educational experience.

He participated in discussions on the latest advancements in techniques, technologies, and patient-care strategies — returning with new insights that bring his patients the most up-to-date and effective treatments in cardiovascular medicine.

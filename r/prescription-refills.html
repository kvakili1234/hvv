Prescription Refills

If you’re calling our office for prescription refills, please allow 72 to hours to fill.

Before leaving your appointment, please be sure to request enough prescription refills to last until your next appointment.

The best way to refill your prescriptions is by contacting your pharmacist first. They would transmit an electronic request to us. This ensures that the information is accurate and medication interactions and/or allergies are verified electronically.

Please contact your pharmacy for prescription refills first. You may also request prescription refills through our patient portal.

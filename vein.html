Endovenous Services

Vein Services

Our Vein Services address both the medical and cosmetic impact of venous disease using office-based, minimally invasive care. We begin with duplex ultrasound to define venous reflux disease and the severity of chronic venous insufficiency, then tailor a stepwise plan. Conservative vein treatments—compression therapy, leg elevation, exercise, and skin care—help relieve swelling and aching while protecting skin. When reflux persists, we use endovenous radiofrequency catheter ablation to close the faulty great or small saphenous veins and reroute flow. Bulging tributaries can be removed through tiny incisions with ambulatory phlebectomy for rapid recovery. Persistent spider and reticular veins are treated with sclerotherapy to gradually fade visible veins. Throughout care, questions are answered through our Varicose Vein FAQs so expectations are clear regarding risks, benefits, and aftercare. With precision imaging, proven techniques, and attentive follow-up, Dr. Vakili’s team restores comfort, reduces swelling, improves appearance, and supports lasting leg health. Relief that lasts.
